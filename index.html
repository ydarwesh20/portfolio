About

A storyteller
in every medium.

I'm Youssef Ali — a media practitioner with a passion for the full craft of visual storytelling. My path started with sound, moved through cinema and direction, and settled — for now — on editing as my sharpest tool.

Chapter 01
Sound &
Radio
It started with audio — the intimacy of a voice, the rhythm of a broadcast, the warmth of a well-produced podcast. Sound taught me that storytelling is felt before it's seen.
Chapter 02
Cinema &
The Frame
Then came the image — framing, directing, the directorial vision. Learning to see the world as a series of shots and stories waiting to be cut together.
Chapter 03
Editing &
Content
Now, editing is where everything converges — where sound, image, and story become one, and a client's idea becomes something that genuinely lands.
